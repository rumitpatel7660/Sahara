About Us

Our Story

Sahara Brass Industries blends industrial-grade processes with traditional finishing techniques. Our parts are engineered to exacting tolerances and finished for a premium look that lasts decades.

Founded in 2005, we have been serving the architectural and interior design community with high-quality brass hardware that combines functionality with aesthetic appeal. Our manufacturing facility in Gujarat, India, employs over 150 skilled craftsmen and engineers.

We specialize in custom manufacturing, allowing architects and designers to bring their unique visions to life. Our quality control process ensures that every product meets international standards.
